Welcome to FAQ section

How do I find recipes based on ingredients?

Simply enter the ingredients you have in the search bar, and we will show you recipes that include those ingredients.

Can I save my favorite recipes?

Yes! You can create an account and save your favorite recipes to access them anytime.

Is Recipe Hunter free to use?

Absolutely! Our platform is free to use, with a growing collection of recipes.

Can I submit my own recipes?

Absolutely! We welcome user-submitted recipes. If you have a recipe you'd like to share, head over to our "Contact Us" page or email RecipeHunter@gmail.com.
